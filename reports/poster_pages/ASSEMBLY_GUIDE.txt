POSTER ASSEMBLY GUIDE
==================================================

Poster: 30.0" x 20.0" landscape
Printed on: 9 A4 landscape pages
Grid: 3 columns x 3 rows
Overlap: 0.5" per shared edge

Arrangement (looking at poster from front):

  [Page  1]  [Page  2]  [Page  3]
  [Page  4]  [Page  5]  [Page  6]
  [Page  7]  [Page  8]  [Page  9]

Instructions:
1. Print poster_tiled.pdf on A4 paper, LANDSCAPE orientation.
2. Lay out pages in the grid above.
3. Overlap shared edges by 0.5" and tape from behind.
4. Trim excess white edges if needed.
